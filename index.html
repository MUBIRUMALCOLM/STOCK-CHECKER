Stock Level Checker

Selling trainers. The item is a pair of trainers in size 7. The shop starts with 50 pairs of trainers.

Note: If the stock is below 15, an alert will display: "Less than 15 pairs remaining, order more stock". If the stock falls below 5, the alert will display: "Less than 5 pairs remaining, order more stock". If the stock is all sold, a message will display: "No stock remaining". If the stock is 15 or above, it will display the current stock quantity plus "Sufficient stock".
